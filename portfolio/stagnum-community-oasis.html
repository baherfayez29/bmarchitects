Stagnum Community Oasis

Cultural | Abu Dhabi, UAE | 2022

The Stagnum Community Oasis is a cultural center designed to serve as a gathering place for the local community while honoring traditional Arabic architectural principles. The project creates a modern interpretation of the traditional oasis, providing shade, water features, and communal spaces in a contemporary context.

The design incorporates sustainable features including passive cooling systems inspired by traditional wind towers, water conservation through innovative recycling systems, and locally-sourced materials that minimize the carbon footprint while connecting to the regional vernacular.

Indoor and outdoor spaces flow seamlessly, creating a series of interconnected areas that can accommodate various community activities from small gatherings to larger cultural events. The central courtyard serves as the heart of the project, featuring water elements that provide both cooling effects and aesthetic value.

Project Details

  • Client: Abu Dhabi Cultural Foundation
  • Size: 3,500 m²
  • Status: Completed 2022
  • Services: Architecture, Landscape Design, Interior Design
