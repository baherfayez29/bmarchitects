Meeting Room Blowup

Interior Design | Dubai, UAE | 2023

This modern meeting space draws inspiration from the flowing forms of sand dunes and the practical wisdom of traditional Arabic design elements. The project focuses on creating a functional yet aesthetically pleasing environment for collaboration and discussion.

The design features natural materials, a neutral color palette with desert-inspired accents, and strategic lighting to create a warm and inviting atmosphere. Traditional Arabic patterns are subtly incorporated into modern furnishings and fixtures.

Acoustic considerations were paramount in the design, with sound-absorbing materials integrated seamlessly into the aesthetic. The space balances openness with privacy, creating zones for different types of interaction.

Project Details

  • Client: Corporate Headquarters
  • Size: 120 m²
  • Status: Completed 2023
  • Services: Interior Design, Furniture Selection, Lighting Design
