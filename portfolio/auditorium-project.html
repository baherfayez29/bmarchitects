Auditorium Project

Commercial | Dubai, UAE | 2023

The Auditorium Project represents a harmonious blend of acoustic excellence and desert-inspired architecture. Designed to host a variety of performances and events, this space combines technical precision with aesthetic beauty inspired by the surrounding landscape.

The auditorium's form takes cues from the undulating sand dunes of the desert, with flowing lines that not only create visual interest but also contribute to the exceptional acoustics of the space. The ceiling design features parametrically designed panels that diffuse and direct sound while evoking the patterns of wind-swept sand.

Materials were carefully selected to balance acoustic requirements with sustainability goals. Local stone and wood are featured prominently, creating a warm atmosphere while connecting the space to its regional context. State-of-the-art lighting systems are integrated seamlessly into the architecture, allowing for dramatic effects that enhance performances.

Project Details

  • Client: Dubai Cultural Authority
  • Size: 2,800 m²
  • Capacity: 650 seats
  • Status: Completed 2023
  • Services: Architecture, Acoustic Design, Lighting Design, Interior Design

Technical Features

Acoustic Excellence

Parametrically designed acoustic panels inspired by sand dune patterns provide optimal sound diffusion and reflection, creating a balanced acoustic environment suitable for various performance types.

Integrated Lighting

Advanced LED lighting systems are seamlessly integrated into the architectural elements, allowing for dynamic lighting scenarios that enhance the performance experience while minimizing energy consumption.

Climate Control

A sophisticated HVAC system maintains optimal comfort while operating efficiently in the desert climate. The design incorporates traditional cooling principles with modern technology for a sustainable approach.
